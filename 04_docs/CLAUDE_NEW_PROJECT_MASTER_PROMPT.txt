============================================================
SIMULATION REQUIREMENTS FOR FIRST BUILD
============================================================

Create a simple but real deterministic simulation core.
Separate simulation from rendering.

State should include:
- day/time
- resources: coins/leaves, water/research, reputation
- tanks
- species populations
- water quality values:
  - oxygen
  - temperature
  - pH
  - ammonia
  - nitrite
  - nitrate
  - cleanliness
- habitat score
- event log

Implement basic loop:
- fish/shrimp/snails produce waste over time
- uneaten food increases waste/ammonia
- ammonia/nitrite/nitrate affect health/habitat score
- plants help oxygen/cleanliness slightly
- cleaning improves cleanliness
- water change reduces ammonia/nitrite/nitrate
- overfeeding creates warning events
- low cleanliness creates warning events

Keep it readable and fun. Do not overcomplicate chemistry yet.
